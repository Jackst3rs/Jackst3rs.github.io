Our Teams

Over in Summerbridge there are plenty of teams to root for! combine these with our famous sports bars and you have a match made in heaven for your favorite tailgate lover!
Show up for just one game or all of them! Instead of playing constantly, as is customary with blaseball, the amount of overlap has forced team leaders to split games into specific days. Making them much more accessible to the average person!
