About Us

We are the Summerbridge Visitors Bureau, we have been working with the officials of Summerbridge for 18 years now bringing the best improvements to the community we can bring!
We are all natives to the Kilmaine region looking to put more of a spotlight on our own favorite vacation spot! We seek to infuse the Summerbridge economy in order to help bring even more smiles to peoples faces.
