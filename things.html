Fun for all ages!


Here in Summerbridge theres a little something for everyone. Whether its you and the folks, or just you yourself! We pride ourselves on having a type of business for everyone.
Starting off in the outskirts, there's plenty of food to keep everyone fed, and with that, theres entertainment too!

The Fairman's Carnival is a weekly fair that shows up nearly every weekend to let you blow off some steam with friends after a hard workweek. It's got fried food, adult drinks, entertainment, and rides to keep everyone in the family busy! Especially the kiddos with all of the fun carnival attractions!
Nearing the end of the night, the carnival turns into a real spectacle with fireworks seen best from the giant ferris wheel near the entrance.


Missed the weekend? Thats ok! Theres still plenty more!
Inner downtown is home to many businesses, most notable being the Fisherman's Marina and Aquarium. At this aquarium, experience the lonely depths of the sea floor in the all-new Sea- Floor Exhibit! There, the Fisherman's boasts to having the most realistic experience of what it would be like to live on the sea floor!
But if the dark adventurous waters aren't your style, feel free to check their more light hearted exhibits! The pirate exhibit is particularly popular with kids and couples alike!
Don't forget to check out the gift shop on your way out!

Still don't know what to do in Summerbridge?
Check out the oldest shop in Summerbridge, a mystical magical store called The Witches Broomstick. Run by the long running Dahlia family for over 50 years now, The Witches Broomstick is where everyone can find something they'll like!
From trinkets for little kids, to even full on broomstick riding lessons for adults, everyone can have a fun time here! And don't miss in the fall time when they start doing Real Pictures with a Real Witch!

If none of these are your style, simply come out and enjoy the sunset or eat at the most fun and fancy restaurants we have to offer!
