Get the most out of your stay with our fun seasonal events!

New years
Celebrated January 1st at midnight with the biggest fireworks seen from our biggest ferris wheel at the carnival!
Sweetest Day
Celebrated February 14th with the heart balloon toss. come with your beloveds to see who can throw the heart balloon further into the fairgrounds!
The Gift Festival
Celebrated December 20-25th with decorating every tree in downtown, then with days of sales and shopping!
Labor day
Celebrated the first Monday in September with a giant town-wide cookout!
Those who are especially skilled are encouraged to come to the town hall with their best dish in hand. Winners of the competition could get up to a thousand dollars!
Fathers day
Celebrated at the third Sunday in June with buy one get one free meals at nearly every restaurant in town!
Mothers day
Celebrated the second Sunday in May, those with mothers they really love are encouraged to join the mother's day dance at the town hall.
Halloween
Celebrated october 31st with a spooky scary parade at 9pm that night. Candy will flood the streets when the floats hit them!
Independance day
Celebrated june 4th with fireworks, a parade, and this year, the opening of a brand new water park at the Sleepy Deer Lodge!
