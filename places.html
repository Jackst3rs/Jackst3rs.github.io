Relax and let your worries melt away at these hotels

Here at Summerbridge, we take your relaxation seriously. Which is why all of our hotels are kept to the highest standards, so you can relax while you enjoy the sights and sounds of our little town.

Looking to come just with you and the love of your life? Well then The Hen's Roost is just for you!
Home to the most beautiful beachside views, The Hen's Roost comes with all sorts of special amenities to make your nights amazing. Along with a beautiful elegant restaurant that is free to all Golden Egg members!
Theres rooms for families here too, with state of the art room noise dampening technology so you can rest assured that your kids can have all the fun they want, without you paying the price.

The hotel is siuated right by the Fisherman's Marina and Aquarium too! So while your nights can be full of fun and romance, your days can be as well with just a few miles in between!


While a beautiful hotel like that is perfect for those looking for that beach getaway, for more rustic types theres also a perfect place for you.

Home to one of the largest forests in the Kilmaine region, campsites are a plenty, but one we wholeheartedly endorse is the Sleepy Deer Lodge. Known more for its urban location in Rafferty, the Sleepy Deer Lodge is somehow even more homey than a log cabin could be!

It features a rustic all wood interior with gorgeous views of the sun setting over the trees on higher floors. It's mascot, the Sleepy Deer himself often time shows up during the breakfast on the lodge's main floor, making it an adorable photo op for families with young kids.
It's perfect for the lovebirds in your life too, with plans to expand into the forest with individual cabins for those looking for a more private experience.

Looking for something even closer to home? Then the William's Bed and Breakfast is for you!

built in the early 1900s, this bed and breakfast exhudes hometown charm. Pictures of family members pepper the walls of the rented rooms, making you feel included in the family, despite them being polite and out of your way except for at breakfast time.

If any of these sound good to you, don't wait until you're already stressed from work to book.
Book today and save yourself the headache!
